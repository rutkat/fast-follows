Fast Follows!

Well done! You've just installed the Fast Follows Chrome Extension developed by Rutkat. Platforms currently support which may change any time in this extension are:

  • 'twitter.com'
  • 'soundcloud.com'
  • 'medium.com'
  • 'quora.com'
  • 'pinterest.com'
  • 'linkedin.com'

Please note that this Chrome Extension is most effective when you already have great-quaility and engaging content on your social media accounts. Also each website will have a limit of follows you can do per day depending on the time the account has existed among other variables. Exceeding this limit may cause your account to be suspended so use at your own risk. I have tested the follows and set the max to 35 for each time you the query button finishes its execution. Additional follows within a 24 hour time period will result in an error and warning from the platform.

Instructions

  1. Login to the platform supported by this extension. Unsupported websites will render the extension icon in gray.
  2. Navigate to user's account then the subpage of the user's followers.
  3. Click on the extension icon in the toolbar to open the popup.
  4. Click the query page button to follow the users visible on the page.
  5. Alternatively, you can follow users by right-clicking in a blank area of the page to enable the "Fast Follow" conext menu.
